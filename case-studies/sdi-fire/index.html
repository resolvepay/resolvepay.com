The SDI Fire Success Story
SDI Fire was founded 1996 with the mission of providing security and fire alarm testing equipment to corporations all around the world.
Initially, SDI Fire only provided a small range of detector testing tools. SDI Fire has since grown from its humble beginnings and now offer a wide array of industrial grade fire alarm testing products from top manufacturers all around the world.
SDI Fire continues to maintain relationships with leading industry associations and committees to ensure that they continue to be knowledgable and well-versed in the latest fire protection and safety standards. Notable clients of SDI Fire include; Case Study: SDI Fire 2 NASA, Disney and Seaworld. Their devices can even be found in Buckingham Palace.
The Challenge of Providing B2B Lending to Their Customers
As told to Resolve by Joe Cagiano, National Sales Manager, SDI Fire SDI Fire was initially searching for a B2B bank or lending company. SDI Fire needed a partner that could help provide B2B lending to their customers to continue to build their relationship with these customers without having to reach out to SDI Fire's distributors to enable this type of service. The top requirements for SDI Fire's new net payment terms partner:
  • Free up working capital
    One of the important selection criteria for SDI Fire was the ability to free up their working capital. SDI Fire understood that by having more working capital they could invest this capital into the company's overall growth.
  • B2B lending to their customers
    SDI Fire needed to have a platform where they could offer net terms to their customers that was fast and easy to use for them and their customers.
  • Ability to reach out for support
    SDI Fire wanted a partner that had the small company feel so they could reach out and talk to a real person if they were running into any issues.
The SDI Fire and Resolve Partnership
SDI Fire had a clear selection criteria when they were looking for a partner that could help solve their B2B net payments problems. They needed a product that is easy to use for both them and their customers.
It was very important for SDI Fire that the credit application process was super easy to navigate and understand. They didn't want a situation where the application process ended up becoming a blocker to their customers.
In Resolve SDI Fire was able to find a solution that was very simple for both SDI Fire and for SDI Fire's customers. In fact, it was so easy that their customers Case Study: SDI Fire 3 didn't really believe SDI Fire when they walked their customers through the application process.
SDI Fire spent time evaluating other competitors in the B2B lending space. But none of these other offerings gave SDI Fire the level of comfort that they needed in order to proceed and sign on with these solution providers. Resolve was able to solve SDI Fire's B2B lending problems while maintaining a very personal relationship. SDI Fire wanted to know that their needs were important and that they could have a direct line of communication with their service provider and Resolve was able to provide that level of personal support to them.
Resolve Helps SDI Fire Improve Profit Margins
Since partnering with Resolve, SDI Fire has seen a marked improvement on their profit margins. SDI Fire's profit margins have increased by 25% in comparison to prior partnering with Resolve.
Historically, their customers would have gone to a distributor so that they could purchase products on net payment terms. Now SDI Fire is able to work directly with these customers. With Resolve, SDI Fire has improved their processes of how they work with their customers. SDI Fire is able to build direct relationships with their customers.
Resolve has taken away any guess work required when providing credit to SDI Fire's customers. In the past, SDI Fire would have to run their own credit checks on a one-to-one basis. It was very difficult for SDI Fire to understand their customers' credit worthiness. The process of evaluating a potential customer's credit worthiness was long (sometimes taking weeks). SDI Fire's potential customers sometimes struggled in their ability to provide trade references which was a key piece to understanding credit worthiness. In these situations in the past, SDI Fire had to go and make an educated guess when deciding whether or not to extend credit to their customers.
SDI Fire no longer has to go through a long vetting process with each customer, they can leverage Resolves ability to take care of all the credit offering decisions and they can focus on growing their business. This has significantly shortened the Case Study: SDI Fire 4 amount of time they require to make credit decisions (from weeks down to hours).
SDI Fire's customers are delighted that the credit application is seamless as the credit product is still billed as SDI Fire, but now it's powered by Resolve. So from this perspective, SDI Fire's customers feel a level of security since they are still purchasing directly from SDI Fire. Their customers love the ability to apply for net terms on SDI Fire's website and get a response back relatively quickly.
SDI Fire plans to increase their partnership with Resolve. Eventually, SDI Fire would like to migrate all of their accounts receivable needs to Resolve. SDI Fire feels like Resolve has been able hit on all the metrics that they care about; increase in profit margins, process simplification and increase in conversions.
